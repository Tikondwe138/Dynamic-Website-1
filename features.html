Leisure Facilities

At GWSC, we offer a range of leisure facilities to enhance your camping and swimming experience. Our facilities include an outdoor cinema, playground, picnic area, and a sports field.

Entertainment

We provide a variety of entertainment options for our guests, including live music, dance performances, and storytelling around the campfire. Join in the fun and create memories that will last a lifetime.

Amenities and Rules

Our site has a range of amenities, including car parking, showers, toilets, and internet access. Please ensure you follow our rules during your stay, including respecting other guests, keeping noise levels down, and leaving the site clean and tidy.
